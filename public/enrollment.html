Enrollment Policy & Agreement

I. ENROLLMENT POLICY

Enrollment at Cebu Sacred Heart College, Inc. (CSHC) is subject to the following terms and conditions. By enrolling, the student and parent/guardian agree to abide by all school policies, rules, and regulations as set forth by the school administration.

II. ACADEMIC STANDARDS

Students are expected to maintain satisfactory academic performance. A student who fails to meet the minimum passing grade requirements may be subject to academic probation or dismissal. Regular attendance is required. Absences beyond the allowable limit may result in a failing grade or disqualification from examinations.

III. PAYMENT OF FEES

All school fees must be paid according to the payment schedule set by the Accounting Department. Failure to settle financial obligations may result in the withholding of academic records, certificates, and clearances. The school reserves the right to refuse enrollment to students with outstanding balances from previous school years.

IV. CONDUCT AND DISCIPLINE

Students are expected to conduct themselves with integrity and respect at all times. Acts of misconduct, dishonesty, disrespect toward teachers and staff, or violation of school rules may result in disciplinary action, suspension, or expulsion as deemed appropriate by the school administration.

V. USE OF SCHOOL FACILITIES

Students are responsible for the proper use and care of school facilities, equipment, and property. Any damage caused by negligence or misconduct will be the financial responsibility of the student and/or parent/guardian.

VI. DATA PRIVACY

In compliance with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), CSHC collects and processes personal information solely for enrollment, academic, and administrative purposes. Student data will not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

VII. HEALTH AND SAFETY

Students must comply with all health and safety protocols set by the school and applicable government agencies. The school reserves the right to implement additional health measures as necessary.

VIII. AMENDMENT OF POLICIES

The school reserves the right to amend, update, or revise these policies at any time. Students and parents/guardians will be duly notified of any changes through official school communication channels.
